# Thesis: NVIDIA (NVDA)
- Growth Driver: Dominant AI chip architecture (Blackwell, Rubin).
- Competitive Moat: Unrivaled software ecosystem (CUDA) and high-performance computing demand.
- Exit Conditions: Fundamental failure in AI demand, sustained revenue decline, technical breakdown below support at $197.32.

# Thesis: Fortinet (FTNT)
- Growth Driver: AI-driven cybersecurity demand and FortiOS integration.
- Competitive Moat: Diversified security platform and high operating efficiency.
- Exit Conditions: Loss of growth momentum, technical breakdown below $88.63.

# Thesis: GE Aerospace (GE)
- Growth Driver: Strong aftermarket service demand and commercial engine backlog.
- Competitive Moat: Massive installed base of engines and strategic U.S. manufacturing advantage.
- Exit Conditions: Significant drop in service volumes, technical breakdown below $211.15.
