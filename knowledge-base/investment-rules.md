# Investment Rules - ThesisVenture

## Buy Rules
All 3 conditions must be met based on team reports:
- Ben's Fundamental Verdict: PASS
- Sarah's Technical Verdict: PASS
- Alex's Sentiment Verdict: PASS

If all 3 PASS → BUY
If 2 PASS, 1 NEUTRAL → BUY (small position)
If any FAIL → Do not buy

## Hold Rules
- Hold as long as the Thesis remains intact
- Short-term price drops are not a reason to sell
- Review every Quarter after earnings

## Sell Rules
- Ben reports Fundamental FAIL (earnings collapse, revenue decline)
- Sarah reports Technical FAIL (breakdown below key support)
- Alex reports Sentiment FAIL (major news threatens thesis)
- Any 2 of 3 reports FAIL → SELL immediately

## Position Sizing
Nick decides position size based on team report strength:
- All 3 PASS strongly → larger position
- Mixed signals (some NEUTRAL) → smaller position
- Nick has full discretion within fund rules

## Risk Management
- No Leverage
- Maintain minimum 10% cash reserve at all times
- Diversify across sectors when possible

## Fund Rules
- Total Capital: $10,000 USD
- Min Cash Reserve: 10% ($1,000) at all times
- Max Positions: 10 stocks

## Portfolio Reporting
Nick must report every week:
- Total Capital: $10,000 USD
- Cash Available: Total - Invested
- Invested Amount: sum of all active positions
- Number of Positions: current / max (10)
- Return: (Current Value - Starting Capital) / Starting Capital x 100%
- Each position: Ticker, Shares, Avg Cost, Current Price, Value, Return

## Position Management
### Adding to Existing Position (Scale In)
- Only add when: thesis confirmed by 2+ consecutive earnings beats
- Maximum total position: 25% of portfolio
- Never average down on a losing position unless thesis is intact

### Trimming Position (Scale Out)  
- Trim when position exceeds 25% of total portfolio value
- Trim when stock is up >100% and fundamentals show deceleration
- Keep at least half the position if thesis still intact

## Rebalancing Rules
- Review portfolio balance every quarter
- No single stock > 25% of portfolio
- No single sector > 40% of portfolio
- If cash drops below 10%, do not make new BUY decisions

## Weekly Checklist
Before making any decision, Nick must confirm:
- [ ] Read all 3 research reports (Ben, Sarah, Alex)
- [ ] Read portfolio/mistakes.md
- [ ] Check current market condition (Bull/Bear/Uncertain)
- [ ] Review existing positions for exit conditions
- [ ] Confirm cash reserve > 10% before any new BUY
