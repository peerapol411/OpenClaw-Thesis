# Investment Rules - ThesisVenture

## Buy Rules
- Fundamental Pass: Positive Revenue and Profit trend
- Valuation Pass: Price reasonable relative to Growth
- Technical Pass: Uptrend or forming a base
- Sentiment Pass: No major news threatening the core Thesis
- Conviction: High or Medium only

## Hold Rules
- Hold as long as the Thesis remains intact
- Short-term price drops are not a reason to sell
- Review every Quarter after earnings

## Sell Rules
- Fundamentals deteriorate significantly
- Revenue or Earnings drop sharply without temporary reason
- Key management departure or Governance issues
- Clearly better opportunity elsewhere (Opportunity Cost)
- Price is significantly overvalued vs Fundamentals

## Position Sizing
- Max 20% per stock
- Max 10 positions
- Start at 5-10%, add when Thesis confirmed

## Risk Management
- No Leverage
- Maintain minimum 10% cash reserve
- Reduce positions if overall market risk is elevated
## Fund Rules
- Total Capital: $1,000 USD
- Max Positions: 10 stocks (max 20% or $200 per stock)
- Min Cash Reserve: 10% ($100) at all times
- Start small: first buy 5-10% of capital per position
- Scale up only when thesis is confirmed by earnings

## Portfolio Reporting
Nick must report the following every week:
- Total Capital: starting $10,000 USD
- Cash Available: Total - Invested
- Invested Amount: sum of all active positions
- Number of Positions: current / max (10)
- Return: (Current Value - Starting Capital) / Starting Capital x 100%
- Each position: Ticker, Shares, Avg Cost, Current Price, Value, Return
