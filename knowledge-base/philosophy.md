# Investment Philosophy - ThesisVenture

## Approach: Wonderful Company at a Fair Price (Buffett/Munger)
- Durable Economic Moat with high pricing power
- High ROE (>15%) and strong Free Cash Flow
- Trustworthy management with skin in the game
- Clear Margin of Safety vs Intrinsic Value

## Universe
- US equities (S&P 500, NASDAQ)
- Tech, Semiconductor, Innovation sectors

## Time Horizon
- 3-5+ years, ideally forever
- Sell only when Moat permanently broken

## Market Conditions
- Bull (S&P above 200MA): Patience, strict Margin of Safety
- Bear (S&P below 200MA): Hunting Season — deploy into discounted moats
- VIX > 30: Scale into existing high-conviction positions

## Avoid
- Value Traps (cheap + dying business)
- High debt without FCF
- FOMO/meme stocks
- Businesses outside Circle of Competence
